# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers coordinate and oversee the release process from code freeze to production deployment. They ensure all release requirements are met, manage release schedules, and facilitate coordination between development, QA, and operations teams.

### Responsibilities
- Plan and schedule release windows and deployment activities
- Ensure all pre-release requirements are met (tests, approvals, documentation)
- Coordinate with DevOps, QA, and development teams during releases
- Manage release notes and communicate changes to stakeholders
- Monitor deployments and coordinate rollback procedures if needed
- Maintain release documentation and post-release reports

### Goals
- Deliver high-quality releases on schedule with minimal disruption
- Reduce deployment risks through thorough preparation and validation
- Maintain clear visibility into release status and readiness

### Typical Communication
- Release planning meetings with engineering and product teams
- Go/no-go decisions with stakeholders before deployments
- Release announcements and post-deployment status updates
- Coordination with support and customer success on release impacts

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads define and oversee quality assurance strategies, ensuring that features meet acceptance criteria and quality standards before release. They coordinate testing efforts and champion quality throughout the development lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategies and QA processes for projects
- Review acceptance criteria and ensure testability
- Coordinate manual and automated testing efforts
- Identify quality risks and gaps in test coverage
- Facilitate bug triage and prioritization with product and engineering
- Ensure quality gates are met before releases
- Maintain test documentation and quality metrics

### Goals
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Improve test coverage and automation
- Reduce cycle time for quality validation

### Typical Communication
- Daily coordination with developers on feature readiness
- Bug triage meetings and quality reviews
- Test plan reviews with product and engineering
- Quality metrics reporting to stakeholders

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers research user needs, design intuitive interfaces, and ensure that products deliver excellent user experiences. They collaborate with product managers and developers to create user-centered solutions.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and prototypes
- Define interaction patterns and information architecture
- Collaborate with developers on implementation feasibility
- Validate designs through user feedback and testing
- Maintain design systems and UI consistency
- Advocate for user needs and accessibility

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and improve task completion rates
- Ensure design consistency across product features

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with product and engineering teams
- User research findings and recommendations
- Collaboration with developers on design implementation
- Usability test results and iteration feedback

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and deployment automation. They enable reliable, efficient delivery of software to production and ensure system reliability and observability.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines and deployment automation
- Manage infrastructure provisioning and configuration
- Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting systems
- Ensure security and compliance in deployment processes
- Troubleshoot production issues and performance bottlenecks
- Collaborate with developers on build and deployment optimization
- Maintain disaster recovery and backup procedures

### Goals
- Enable fast, safe, automated deployments
- Maximize system uptime and reliability
- Improve observability and incident response capabilities

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure planning with engineering and product teams
- Incident response coordination during outages
- Deployment readiness reviews with release managers
- Platform updates and maintenance windows announcements

---

## Stakeholder (Customer/User Champion)

### Role Summary
Stakeholders represent customer interests, provide business context, and validate that solutions meet real user needs. They may be internal champions, customer representatives, or product sponsors who ensure alignment with business goals.

### Responsibilities
- Provide input on business requirements and priorities
- Validate solutions against customer and business needs
- Participate in user research and feedback sessions
- Review and approve major feature decisions and trade-offs
- Communicate product updates to customer or business teams
- Escalate customer concerns and feature requests
- Support go-to-market and adoption efforts

### Goals
- Ensure product delivers business value and meets customer needs
- Maintain alignment between development and business objectives
- Maximize customer satisfaction and adoption

### Typical Communication
- Feature validation sessions and acceptance reviews
- Business requirement discussions with product managers
- Feedback sessions on prototypes and beta features
- Updates to customer-facing teams on roadmap and releases
